Welcome to Coca Cola

Coca Cola is an American beverage company that owns Fanta, Sprite, Minute Maid and many more brands and is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia. The company was first started in 1886 by a pharmacist by the name of John Smith Pemberton and today is estimated to be worth 79.2 billion dollars. The company's main soda, Coca Cola, is the #1 most popular drink around the world today and an estimated 1.9 billion serving are comsumed everyday globally. The distinct bottle can be recognized all over the world as the company continues to grow and distribute its products world-wide.
